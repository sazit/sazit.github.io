Projects

Western Association of Computing and Mathematics (WACM)
2019, 2020

WACM was a club I founded and ran with the help of a number of amazing people at Western Sydney University. I layed the groundwork and aimed for the club to be the representative society for the new School of Computing, Data and Mathematical Sciences.
Detecting Australian Racism in Social Networks
2019

As a recipient of the Vacation Research Scholarship from the Australian Mathematicsl Sciences Institute, I used natural language processing techniques to analyse tweets made my Australian users in the hopes of finiding patterns of racist sentiment.
Predictive Modelling for Spotting Sepsis
2019

In my final semester for the unit 301111 Predictive Modelling, I worked on analysing data of patients in the emergency department in hospitals and the limited information received in that stage can be used to predict the severity of their illness, with a particular focus on Sepsis.
Simulation of Large Scale Mapping with Tidbinbilla's Telescope
2018

As a recipient of the Summer Research Scholarship from the Western Sydney University, I looked into simulating where telescopes would point on the map of the universe in relation to how they were set up here on Earth.
Setting up a Coffee Cart
2018

For this consulting project, me and the team provided reccomendations to a non-profit organization on how they could assist migrant women from Sudan start their own coffee cart shop assess the feasibility on making it into a sustainable business.
